About

Through periods of drought and famine, WorldKind Relief ensures that families across the Horn are nourished and sustained. WorldKind Relief extends a helping hand with it’s food program by providing emergency food baskets and distributing meat for holiday feasts in communities in Ethiopia, Kenya, Somalia, and Sudan. This food distribution gives families essential nutrition in times of crisis and allows them time to create proper resiliency plans for the future.

WorldKind Reliefis a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit, tax-exempt organization based in Minnesota. All donations are tax-deductible. Tax ID #41-1989714.
